The villa

A home engineered around how long, how clear, and how calm you want to live.

Sosiego sits on 1,096 m² of pine-shaded ground in Pinares de San Antón, eight minutes from Málaga centre. The plot writes the brief: mature trees stay, the slope becomes program, the morning sun decides where you wake up. We adapt the project to the plot, not the plot to the project.

Three principles inform every square metre — the body you move through, the mind you sharpen, the soul you settle.

Body

Move. Recover. Repeat.

Indoor and outdoor gym, lap pool, cold plunge, sauna, infrared cabin. Movement built into the architecture, not added as an afterthought.

Mind

Think better. Rest deeper.

Library, meditation room, cinema, a Zen garden carved between the pines. Light studied for the actual orientation, never assumed.

Soul

Slow down. Tune in.

KM0 vegetable garden, olive grove, fire pit, summer kitchen. The Andalusian table, returned to the centre of the home.
