The artifact

Half is built into the wall.
The other half is yours.

Every Sadala home is delivered with one hand-cut stone, taken from the same Andalusian quarry that supplied the house. The stone is cleaved.

One half is set into a wall niche during construction — visible, finished, permanent. The other half is yours, in a wooden box. Both engraved with the home's number.

The stone, when struck with a small bronze rod, rings. Each home's stone has its own pitch.
