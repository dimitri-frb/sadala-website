Why we are not a developer

Sadala is a studio. The difference is everything.

A developer optimises for repetition. They buy plots that fit a plan. They paint the marketing first and the walls last. They aren't on site after the keys are handed over.

A studio works the other way around. The plot writes the brief. The plan adapts to the site, the slope, the trees that were already there. One villa at a time. Built by people who live nearby. For buyers we'll meet over coffee, not over email.

This is not a scaleable business. That's not an apology — it's the entire point.

never reused a plan. never will.
