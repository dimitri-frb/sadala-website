Sosiego · the chronology

From a plot to a home, in public.

2024 · OCT
Plot identified

1,096 m² in Pinares de San Antón. Eight stone pines on the south side, all to be preserved. The plot wrote the brief before the architect saw a sheet of paper.

2024 · DEC
First sketch

A pencil drawing on tracing paper. The kitchen window already pointed east — defined by where the pines threw morning shadows.

2025 · APR
Project approved

Building permit issued. Zero variance from the original concept. The plot decides — and the municipality, that day, agreed.

2025 · NOV
Groundbreaking

First spade in the ground on a Wednesday. Three pines we feared we'd lose all stayed.

2026 · APR · TODAY
Cantilever poured

Week 18 of 134. The structure that defines the southern terrace is in place. From here on, the house starts looking like the renders.

2026 · OCT
Watertight shell

Walls, roof, openings. The interior fit-out begins.

2027 · Q3
Keys handed over

Delivery. Olive grove planted. The kitchen takes its first morning light.
