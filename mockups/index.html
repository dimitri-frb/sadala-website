A bigger swing - beyond the four

A homepage redesigned around a single, ownable brand idea: The Sadala Chord. Each home is numbered (N° 01, N° 02…), has a hand-cleaved stone with its own resonant note, and a field recording from its plot. Every Sadala home is one note in a chord that grows with the studio.

Option 6 · brand-led

The Sadala Chord

Numbered vintages (N° 02). A hand-cleaved stone for every home, struck like a bell - F♯ for Sosiego, D for Origen. A playable chord. A real audio experience on the homepage. The most distinctive direction by far.
