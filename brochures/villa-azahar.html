The experience

A villa designed to embrace its surroundings

Among century-old pines with views over Málaga, Villa Azahar is everything a generic development is not: irreplaceable, rooted in its place, designed for how you actually want to live. The outdoor spaces have been conceived as a series of distinct yet connected rooms, each responding to the topography and the rhythms of daily life.

Infinity pool

The horizon starts here. An overflow pool designed to dissolve into the views over Málaga and the Mediterranean, framed by stone terracing and native planting.

Fire pit

A gathering point under the open sky. Where the Málaga night, the scent of the pines and the warmth of the fire create a setting no interior can replicate.

Mirador

An elevated terrace at the heart of the plot. The place from which you understand why this villa exists here and nowhere else. For perfect summers and sunny winters.

Outdoor kitchen

An outdoor kitchen and dining area integrated seamlessly into the landscape. Designed for long lunches, late dinners and everything in between.

Meditation garden

A curated journey through century-old pines and native Mediterranean vegetation. A space of calm, beauty and connection with the natural environment.

Reading paradise

A sheltered corner within the garden, conceived as a place of quiet and retreat. Protected by the shade of the pines and the silence of the urbanisation.

The architecture

An architecture that listens to the site

The architecture is the work of a practice chosen for one defining quality: the ability to listen to a site and respond with precision and sensitivity. Stone, wood and glass come together to create spaces that feel as natural as the landscape around them. No element is arbitrary. Every volume, every opening, every material has been chosen to maximise light, views and the connection between inside and out.

The villa

Designed for daily life. Built to last.

Natural stone floors, solid wood joinery and floor-to-ceiling glazing create spaces that are simultaneously refined and deeply comfortable.

Optional upgrades

Home cinema · Gym & wellness with sauna · Indoor pool & spa. Each option designed in collaboration with the interior design team for a seamless aesthetic. Available for personalisation during the construction phase.
