The vision

A volume of perforated corten steel, floating above the hillside like a bridge between earth and sky. The living roof dissolves the boundary between architecture and landscape, while the structure opens entirely to the garden below.

Living green roof

Flow81

Villa El Cantal is designed entirely by Flow81, an award-winning experiential architecture lab based in Málaga founded by Arregui & Merino. They have shared the podium with Pritzker Prize legends like Foster + Partners, Zaha Hadid, and Tadao Ando. Their structures are born from the terrain itself - spaces that reconnect with nature and engage all five senses.

Location

The new luxury

Built on the mountains of Málaga with sweeping views across the city and the Mediterranean, Villa El Cantal occupies one of the most privileged positions in Málaga Este.

The site sits within Pinares de San Antón, a protected residential enclave surrounded by century-old pine forests. 24-hour security, a private tennis club and a private social club create a self-contained world of calm.
