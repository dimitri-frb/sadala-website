The concept

This is not another luxury villa. It is a home engineered around a single question: what if the space you live in made you a better, healthier, clearer version of yourself?

Health is the new luxury. San Antón 50 is conceived as an instrument for living longer, clearer, calmer. Three pillars inform every square metre: the body you move through, the mind you sharpen, the soul you settle.

01 · Body
Move. Recover. Repeat.

Indoor gym that opens to the pines. TRX station suspended beneath the canopy. Sauna and cold plunge as one circuit. A state-of-the-art infinity pool for morning laps. KM0 vegetables from the garden.

02 · Mind
Think better. Rest deeper.

Neuroarchitecture at every scale. A home office with a walking treadmill and standing desk. A master bedroom terrace with red light therapy. Materials that regulate, not stimulate.

03 · Soul
Slow down. Tune in.

A Japanese meditation garden below the main terrace. A fire pit at the edge of the forest. Olive trees older than the house. The silence of century-old pines.

The architecture of wellness.

Neuroarchitecture is the discipline of designing buildings that work with human biology rather than against it. Every orientation, opening, material and circulation choice at San Antón 50 was considered through that lens.

Circadian light

Morning sun in the east, amber at dusk. Your body knows the hour.

Acoustic silence

The loudest sound is the wind in the pines.

Natural materials

Stone, solid wood, lime plaster. Surfaces that breathe.

Effortless flow

No corridors. Every path invites you to move a little more.
