The concept

Not a villa. A home engineered for the life you've been postponing.

Sosiego · sosiego (n.) · a state of deep calm and clarity

Health is the new luxury. Sosiego is conceived as an instrument for living longer, clearer, calmer. Three pillars inform every square metre: the body you move through, the mind you sharpen, the soul you settle.

01 · Body
Move. Recover. Repeat.

Indoor gym that opens to the pines. TRX beneath the canopy. Sauna and cold plunge as one circuit. Infinity pool for morning laps. KM0 vegetables from the garden.

02 · Mind
Think better. Rest deeper.

Neuroarchitecture at every scale. A home office with a walking treadmill. A master terrace with red light therapy. Materials that regulate, not stimulate.

03 · Soul
Slow down. Tune in.

A Japanese meditation garden below the main terrace. A fire pit at the edge of the forest. Olive trees older than the house. The silence of century-old pines.

The architecture of wellness.

Neuroarchitecture is the discipline of designing buildings that work with human biology rather than against it. Every orientation, opening and material at Sosiego was considered through that lens.

Circadian light

Morning sun in the east, amber at dusk. Your body knows the hour.

Acoustic silence

The loudest sound is the wind in the pines.

Natural materials

Stone, solid wood, lime plaster. Surfaces that breathe.

Effortless flow

No corridors. Every path invites you to move.
