The plot.

Plot

Trees preserved

Orientation
East-facingMorning sun, afternoon shade through the canopy

Eight stone pines, older than the road. A three-metre fall from the entrance to the south corner. A sound meter reading of 32 decibels at noon, eight minutes from Málaga centre. The plot wrote the brief before the architect saw a sheet of paper.

Every decision in chapter 02 follows from chapter 01.

The craft.

01
Stone
Cantera de Mijas

Granitic stone cut and finished within fifteen kilometres of the plot. Same quarry, same craftsmen, same week the foundation pours.

02
Native garden
A nursery in Nerja

Olive saplings, Mediterranean herbs, native grasses. Selected with a horticulturist from Casabermeja who plants the kitchen garden by hand before the keys are handed over.

03
Wood
A mill in Ronda

Solid oak from the Sierra de las Nieves, milled to plank by the same family that has run the saw for three generations.
