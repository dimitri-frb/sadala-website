The experience

Body. Mind. Soul.

Health is the new luxury. Sosiego is conceived as an instrument for living longer, clearer, calmer. Three pillars inform every square metre: the body you move through, the mind you sharpen, the soul you settle.

01 · Body
Move. Recover. Repeat.
Indoor gym that opens to the pines. A TRX station suspended beneath the canopy. Sauna and cold plunge integrated as a single circuit. A state-of-the-art infinity pool designed for morning laps. A kitchen garden for food that travels metres, not miles.
02 · Mind
Think better. Rest deeper.
Neuroarchitecture at every scale: circadian light, acoustic silence, intuitive flow. A home office equipped with a walking treadmill and a standing desk. A master terrace fitted with red light therapy. Surfaces and materials chosen to regulate, not stimulate.
03 · Soul
Slow down. Tune in.
A Japanese meditation garden below the main terrace. A fire pit at the edge of the forest. Olive trees older than the house. The silence of century-old pines. Time, space, and the rare luxury of doing nothing at all.

The conditions for a longer life

Andalucía is one of Europe's last places where longevity isn't a project. It's the culture. 320 days of sun. The Mediterranean diet at your doorstep. Michelin-starred restaurants and family tabernas, both. Air filtered by century-old pines.

Pinares de San Antón is one of the most private residential enclaves in Málaga Este. 24-hour security, a private tennis club, a private social club. Silence. Altitude. A climate the body recognises.

The centre of Málaga, 10 minutes. The Mediterranean, 5. The airport, 20. Trails into the Montes, from your door.
