Where it stands

Nestled among century-old pines with panoramic views over Málaga and the Mediterranean, Pinares de San Antón offers a rare combination of natural beauty, absolute privacy and effortless proximity to the city.

One of the most prestigious private residential enclaves in Málaga Este. 24-hour security, a private tennis club and a private social club create a self-contained world of calm and exclusivity.

The centre of Málaga is 10 minutes away. The Mediterranean is 5 minutes away. Everything else stays outside.
